Artist Statement

Color is the chief motivator of my Art
I Love Color.

Each color has intrinsic meaning & is a reflection of thought and emotion.
My work is meant to reveal myself. I began making art as gifts for my friends, so my work is an expression of loyalty and care for my community. Ultimately, I want it to reflect the universal truths that influence our lives. I strive to poke and prod at the subconscious minds of my audience.
My physical Media include acrylic inks & paints, marker, bubbles, gunpowder, sculpture, metal, bone and found objects. Metaphysically my media are the elements of nature. Air brings about the bubbles, Fire is found in gunpowder, Earth provides the bone, metal and found objects. Water is the basis of my inks and paints. With these elements, I create worlds to grab your attention. I labor so that you may feel, be it complete felicity or terror.
I want you to feel that which has been suppressed.
